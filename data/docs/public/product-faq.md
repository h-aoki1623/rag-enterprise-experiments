# Product FAQ

## General Questions

### What platforms does CloudSync Pro support?
CloudSync Pro supports AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and on-premise data centers. It can synchronize data across any combination of these platforms.

### How is SecureVault different from other password managers?
SecureVault is designed for enterprise use with features like role-based access control, audit logging, secret rotation, and integration with CI/CD pipelines. It supports both human users and machine-to-machine authentication.

### Can I try the products before purchasing?
Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial for all our products. Contact sales@acme-corp.example.com to get started.

## Technical Questions

### What is the uptime SLA for CloudSync Pro?
We guarantee 99.9% uptime for CloudSync Pro. This translates to less than 9 hours of downtime per year.

### Does AnalyticsDash support custom data sources?
Yes, AnalyticsDash supports custom data sources through our SDK. You can connect to any database, API, or file-based data source.

### Is there an API available?
All our products have comprehensive REST APIs and SDKs available in Python, JavaScript, Java, and Go.

## Pricing Questions

### What pricing models do you offer?
We offer subscription-based pricing with monthly and annual options. Volume discounts are available for enterprise customers.

### Is there a free tier?
CloudSync Pro and AnalyticsDash offer limited free tiers for small teams (up to 5 users). SecureVault is available only as a paid product due to security requirements.
