# IT Security Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes information security requirements for all Acme Corporation employees, contractors, and third parties with access to company systems.

## Password Requirements

### Password Complexity
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Cannot contain username or common words
- Cannot reuse last 10 passwords

### Password Management
- Change passwords every 90 days
- Use the company-provided SecureVault for password storage
- Never share passwords via email or chat

## Access Control

### Principle of Least Privilege
Employees should only have access to systems and data necessary for their job functions. Access requests must be submitted through the IT ticketing system.

### Account Deprovisioning
When an employee leaves the company, their access is revoked within 24 hours. Former employees should not retain any company data or credentials.

## Device Security

### Company Devices
- Full disk encryption must be enabled
- Automatic screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Anti-malware software must be running
- Only approved software from the software catalog

### Personal Devices (BYOD)
- Must be enrolled in Mobile Device Management (MDM)
- Company data must be stored in approved apps only
- IT reserves the right to remotely wipe company data

## Network Security

### VPN Usage
- VPN is required when accessing company resources from outside the office
- Use the approved VPN client only
- Do not use public Wi-Fi without VPN

### Guest Network
Visitors should use the "Acme-Guest" network. The guest network is isolated from the corporate network.

## Incident Response

### Reporting Security Incidents
Report all security incidents immediately to:
- Email: security@acme-corp.example.com
- Phone: +1-555-SEC-RITY (24/7 hotline)
- Slack: #security-incidents

### What to Report
- Suspicious emails or phishing attempts
- Lost or stolen devices
- Unauthorized access attempts
- Malware infections
- Data breaches or leaks

## Compliance

Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
