# Employee Handbook

## Welcome to Acme Corporation

This handbook provides essential information about your employment at Acme Corporation. Please read it carefully and keep it for future reference.

## Working Hours

### Standard Hours
Our standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Core hours (when all employees should be available) are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

### Flexible Work
We offer flexible working arrangements. Employees may adjust their start and end times within reason, subject to manager approval. Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Annual Leave
- Years 1-3: 15 days per year
- Years 4-7: 20 days per year
- Years 8+: 25 days per year

Unused PTO can be carried over up to 5 days to the following year.

### Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

### Holidays
We observe 11 company holidays per year. The holiday schedule is published in December for the following year.

## Benefits

### Health Insurance
We provide comprehensive health insurance including medical, dental, and vision coverage. Coverage begins on your first day of employment. The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 50% for dependents.

### 401(k) Plan
Employees are eligible to participate in our 401(k) plan from day one. The company matches contributions up to 4% of salary.

### Professional Development
Each employee has an annual budget of $2,000 for professional development, including courses, conferences, and certifications.

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct and treat colleagues with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

### Confidentiality
Employees must protect company confidential information. Do not share sensitive data with unauthorized parties, including family members or friends.

### IT Security
- Use strong passwords and enable 2FA on all accounts
- Report suspicious emails to security@acme-corp.example.com
- Do not install unauthorized software on company devices
